BLOCK Element!!!

  1. A Block level element, by default, takes up all the space to the right and to the left, internal to its parent
  2. A BLOCK CAN have height and width
  3. NOTHING can ever be on either side (internal to the parent) because its 100%. If you change the width, it will just add margin. Blocks will ignore margin you add, because they're already 100%
  4. Can be > 100%
  1. Respect left and right padding, left and right margin but will ignore top and bottom,
  2. Couldn't care less what is left or right.
  3. With a few exceptions (namely images) you CANNOT tell an inline element how big to be.
  4. Will happily ignore all block level rules unless forced to do so.
